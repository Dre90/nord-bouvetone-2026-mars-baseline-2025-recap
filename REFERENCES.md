## About Baseline

Baseline is an initiative by the W3C WebDX Community Group that provides clear signals about which web platform features work across all major browsers (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari). A feature becomes "Baseline Newly available" when it ships in the last stable version of all major browsers. After 30 months it moves to "Baseline Widely available".
